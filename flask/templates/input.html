VanGo: find art which speaks to you.

VanGo is a personal art curator which searches tens of thousdands of painting and drawings from British Museum's collection to find those that best match your interests. In the field below, type in a word that you'd like us to find.

So far, we have matched around 900 words, mostly nouns, such as, e.g. "flower", "tree", "deity", "river" and we're working on adding more, so just try a few. Have fun!
